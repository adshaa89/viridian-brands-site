Our team

Kyle Adams

Co-founder & President

Capital and company-building: Kyle is co-founder and president of Viridian Brands, where he leads Viridian Capital and the portfolio's financial engine, how each brand is funded, structured, and built to last. He is the founder of KKA Advisors, his Austin-based venture firm backing early-stage technology companies from Arrive Logistics to OpenAI. Aditya Sharma

Founder & CEO

Brand and commercial: Aditya founded Viridian Brands and is its CEO. He spent his career learning to build consumer health brands at the two companies that do it best, Procter & Gamble and Amazon. At P&G he built brands from formulation to national retail, including Align Probiotics. At Amazon he went on to run multi-billion-dollar businesses across Health, Household, and Personal Care, shaping how millions of people discover and buy everyday health products. He has seen consumer health from every side, the lab, the brand, and the shelf, and started Viridian to build the next generation of science-led brands with that depth behind it.
