Common questions

What is Viridian Brands?

Viridian Brands is an operator-led holding company that builds and backs science-led consumer health brands. It brings the team, capital, and operating discipline to build a brand well and get it to market faster. It is based in Austin, Texas, and its brands include Serene Biotics and Viridian Medical Foods.

Who founded Viridian Brands?

Viridian Brands was founded by Aditya Sharma, its CEO, who ran multi-billion-dollar businesses across Health, Household, and Personal Care at Amazon and built brands at Procter & Gamble including Align Probiotics, and Kyle Adams, its president, who built Sagacious Consultants to more than 300 people and sold it to Accenture, and founded the Austin venture firm KKA Advisors.

What brands does Viridian Brands own?

Serene Biotics, a gut-brain supplement for high-performing adults launching on Amazon in 2026, and Viridian Medical Foods, a medical food for age-related loss of strength, in development. Each brand stands on its own name.

Does Viridian Brands invest in or partner with consumer health founders?

Yes. Viridian Brands builds its own brands and backs founders it believes in, sharing the risk and the upside, and collaborates with operators outside its portfolio where its network can help. It works with investors, co-investors, and acquisition or JV partners in consumer health. The fastest way to reach the team is hello@viridianbrands.com.

Is Viridian Brands a consulting firm?

No. Viridian Brands is an operating company and holding company, not a consultancy. It builds and owns consumer health brands and backs founders with capital and hands-on operating support, rather than selling advisory hours.

Is Viridian Brands affiliated with Viridian Therapeutics or Viridian Nutrition?

No. Viridian Brands is an independent operator-led holding company for consumer health brands, based in Austin, Texas. It is not affiliated with Viridian Therapeutics (a biopharmaceutical company) or Viridian Nutrition (a UK supplements brand).
