Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Viridian Brands LLC respects your privacy. This is a simple informational website, and we keep data collection to a minimum.

Information we collect

We do not collect personal information through this website. There are no accounts, sign-up forms, newsletters, e-commerce, or advertising trackers. This site is served through Cloudflare (content delivery and security) and GitHub Pages (static hosting). Like any web host, these services automatically process limited technical data, including your IP address and browser type, to deliver the site and protect it against abuse. Cloudflare may set a security cookie strictly to operate the service. We do not use this data to identify or track you.

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand aggregate traffic, such as page views and referring sites. It is privacy-first: it sets no cookies, does not fingerprint visitors, does not track you across sites, and does not collect personal data. It loads a small script from Cloudflare for this purpose. Apart from that script, the site's fonts and code are embedded directly in the page and load no other third-party resources.

If you contact us

If you email us at hello@viridianbrands.com, we use your message and email address only to respond. We do not add you to any marketing list.

What we do not do

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for marketing, and we do not run advertising or behavioral tracking on this site.

Children

This site is intended for a business audience and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top reflects the current version.
